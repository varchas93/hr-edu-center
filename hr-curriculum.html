Comprehensive HR Curriculum

This structured program takes learners from foundational HR principles to advanced strategic HR management.

HRM Module 1: Introduction to Human Resources Management

  • Definition and Evolution of HRM
  • Key Functions of HR (Administrative vs. Strategic)
  • Role of HR in Organizational Success
  • HR Ethics and Professional Standards
  • HR Compliance and Legal Framework

Module 2: Talent Acquisition & Recruitment

  • Workforce Planning & Job Analysis
  • Writing Effective Job Descriptions
  • Sourcing Strategies
  • Candidate Screening Techniques
  • Employer Branding
  • Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion
  • Recruitment Metrics & Analytics

Module 3: Onboarding & Retention

  • Onboarding Best Practices (Pre-boarding to 90-Day Plan)
  • Employee Engagement Strategies
  • Retention Techniques & Turnover Analysis
  • Career Pathing & Succession Planning
  • Exit Interviews & Offboarding

Module 4: Performance Management

  • Setting SMART Goals & OKRs
  • Performance Appraisal Methods (360 Feedback, MBO, BARS)
  • Continuous Performance Management
  • Coaching & Feedback Techniques
  • Handling Poor Performance & PIPs (Performance Improvement Plans)

Module 5: Learning & Development (L&D)

  • Training Needs Analysis (TNA)
  • Designing Effective Training Programs
  • E-Learning & Digital Learning Platforms
  • Leadership Development & High-Potential (HiPo) Programs
  • Measuring Training ROI (Kirkpatrick Model)

Module 6: Compensation & Benefits

  • Job Evaluation & Salary Benchmarking
  • Pay Structures (Base Pay, Variable Pay, Bonuses)
  • Employee Benefits (Health, Retirement, Wellness Programs)
  • Equity Compensation (Stock Options, ESOPs)
  • Pay Transparency & Pay Equity

Module 7: Employee Relations & Workplace Culture

  • Conflict Resolution & Mediation
  • Handling Grievances & Disciplinary Actions
  • Workplace Policies (Remote Work, Code of Conduct)
  • Building a Positive Organizational Culture
  • HR’s Role in Change Management

Module 8: HR Analytics & Data-Driven Decision Making

  • HR Analytics & Data-Driven Decision Making
  • People Analytics Tools (Power BI, Tableau, HRIS)
  • Predictive Analytics in HR
  • Data Privacy & GDPR Compliance

Module 9: Strategic HR Management

  • Aligning HR Strategy with Business Goals
  • HR as a Business Partner
  • Workforce Planning & Future of Work Trends (AI, Gig Economy)
  • Mergers & Acquisitions (HR Due Diligence)
  • HR’s Role in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Module 10: Global HR & Emerging Trends

  • International HRM (Expatriate Management, Cultural Sensitivity)
  • HR in the Digital Age (AI, Automation, Chatbots)
  • The Future of HR (Hybrid Work, Gen Z Workforce, Skills-Based Hiring)
  • HR Certifications (SHRM, PHR, CIPD)
