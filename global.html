Module 10: Global HR & Emerging Trends

Concept Explanations

International HRM

Expatriate Management: Comprehensive process covering selection, preparation, support, and reintegration of employees working abroad with cross-cultural training and specialized compensation packages.

Cultural Sensitivity: Understanding cultural differences through frameworks like Hofstede's dimensions, addressing communication styles, decision-making norms, and business customs.

HR in the Digital Age

AI in HR: Transforming recruitment through resume screening, predictive analytics for turnover, chatbots for inquiries, and personalized learning recommendations.

Automation & Chatbots: Streamlining processes with automated payroll, self-service portals, digital onboarding, and 24/7 virtual assistants for employee support.

The Future of HR

Hybrid Work: Flexible models combining remote and office work with digital tools, results-oriented metrics, and inclusive practices.

Gen Z Workforce: Digital natives valuing purpose, social responsibility, frequent feedback, and work-life integration.

Skills-Based Hiring: Shift from credentials to competencies using assessments, micro-credentials, and internal mobility frameworks.

HR Certifications

SHRM: Society for HR Management certifications (CP for operational, SCP for strategic roles) with global competency focus.

PHR: US-focused Professional in HR credential covering technical/operational HR areas.

CIPD: UK-based Chartered Institute with foundation (Level 3) to advanced (Level 7) certifications.
